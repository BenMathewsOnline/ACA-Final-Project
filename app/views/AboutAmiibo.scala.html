Amiibo are figures and cards made by Nintendo for the Nintendo Switch, 3DS, and Wii U. They Were originally made for Super Smash Bros. for the Wii U and 3DS, but have since been made for other Nintendo properties, such as Super Mario Odyssey, Legend of Zelda, Metroid, Kirby, and Animal Crossing. Third party publishers have also made Amiibo for games like Shovel Knight.

Amiibo contain an NFC (Near Field Communication) chip that allows Amiibo to communicate to Nintendo software. You can unlock items or can save characters to Amiibo and use them elsewhere.

This website allows you to keep track of your collection.
